Our Coffee

  • Fuel your day with our exceptional coffee services. We pride ourselves in providing the highest quality, ethically-sourced beans that are freshly roasted to order. Our extensive menu caters to all taste buds, featuring everything from bittersweet dark or medium Sagada to bold and nutty Kalinga and smooth Cordillera Arabica.

  • We source our Coffee Beans directly from the local Coffee Farmers of the highlands of the Philippines. From the ranges of Cordillera up to the beautiful mountains in Mindanao. Our Coffee Beans are proudly Philippine produce.
