Our Products

Little Farmer's Coffee prides itself in the several blends of coffee beans gathered from the highlands. Each of them have been roasted in different blends to cater to every taste and palate of every customer.

Use the image map below to check out the current available beans. (Menu Taken from Metro Gaisano lucena Branch)

Our Coffee

  • Benguet

    • Original Blend of Dark Roasted Highlands Robusta and Excelsa Beans. Bold in flavor, very Aromatic and Less Acidic.

  • Benguet Barako

    • Mix of both Highland Robusta and Barako (Liberica) Beans with Strong and Distinct flavor that the Philippine Highland produce has to offer.

  • Sagada

    • Pure Highland Arabica with Pronounced bittersweet taste and sweet aroma. Choice of Dark and Medium Roast.

  • Mt. Apo

    • 100% Premium Arabica. Elevated dried process. Distinct taste with acclaimed aroma. Citrus Fruity and Nutty Flavor notes with hints of Brown Sugar.

  • Kalinga

    • Pure Robusta Beans with acclaimed aroma and taste. Bold and Nutty taste with High caffeine content. Choice of Dark or Medium Roast.
